AA Music Player

AAMusicPlayer demonstrates all the basic functionalities of a media player (and then some) with a GUI based on the Qt5 framework, platform support on both Linux Ubuntu and Mac OSX, and media support of all types supported by Qt's plat-form specific media backends (GStreamer on Linux). It is especially suitable for media-tag (Title, Artist, Album) editing.

  2. taglib 1.9.1 (latest version).
