Playlist

The playlist is a collection of media files that we are currently playing. The playlist area has columns to display the song's Name, Artist, Album and Length information, based on the current file's metadata. Five checkboxes/buttons provide different playlist options including Repeat, Loop, Shuffle, Save and Delete the playlist. The name of the playlist is also shown.

01 Play/Pause Button

Play/Pause button starts or pause the playback of the curent media file.


02 Stop Button

Stop button ceases the current media file.


03 Next & Back Buttons

Next and Back buttons skip to the next or previous songs in the playlist.


04 Volumn Button & Slider

Volumn button and the slider besides it mutes, raises or decreases the volumn.


05 Progress Slider

Slider shows the progress of the playback, which could be manually dragged to advance/rewind the song.
